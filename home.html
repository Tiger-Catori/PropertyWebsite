What we provide for you

Single Buy-to-Let

A single buy-to-let property refers to an investment strategy where an individual purchases a property to rent it out to a single tenant or family, as opposed to multiple unrelated tenants. This is the simplest strategy and comes with lower maintenance and management costs. 

HMO

The demand for affordable housing, especially in urban and university-centric locales, often makes HMOs a sought-after rental choice for both students and professionals. Investors can further enhance the property’s value and rental income by upgrading common areas or individual rooms. 

Serviced Accomodation

For investors, off-market properties present a golden opportunity to realize significant savings. Without the customary competition and public glare associated with open market sales, the path to acquisition is often smoother and less costly.
